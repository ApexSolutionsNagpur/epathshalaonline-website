SECTION 1: ACADEMIC QUALITY & ATTENTION

FAQ 1: Will my child get enough individual attention?
Answer:
Yes. Individual attention is one of the core strengths of ePathshala Online CBSE School.
Our classes are conducted in small, focused groups, allowing teachers to understand each student’s learning pace, strengths, and areas needing support.
In addition, we provide:
Personalized academic monitoring
Dedicated doubt-clearing sessions
Continuous performance tracking
Mentoring support when required
Every student is seen, heard, and guided.

Left: crowded traditional classroom

SECTION 2: SCREEN TIME & HEALTH

FAQ 3: Will there be any adverse effect due to screen exposure?
Answer:
No. Our learning model is carefully designed to ensure healthy and balanced screen usage.
Live class duration: Only 2.5 to 3 hours per day
Classes are conducted in focused sessions with breaks
Students study recorded content at convenient intervals
Project work and offline activities are integrated
This reduces fatigue and improves learning effectiveness.
